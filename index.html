NSDR

In the hustle and bustle of modern life, genuine relaxation has become crucial. Our app introduces non-sleep deep rest technology, allowing you to achieve profound relaxation in a short period. It's not just about closing your eyes; it's a holistic recovery that enables you to recharge in brief intervals, preparing you to face the upcoming challenges.

Visual Focus

Enhancing focus and visual concentration is a primary objective of our app. Through visual gaze training, we guide you through a series of simple yet effective eye movements designed to strengthen your visual focus and mental concentration. This not only aids in improving work efficiency but also reduces eye fatigue, keeping you more focused during work and study.

Binary Beat

Our app incorporates 40Hz binaural beats technology, a scientifically proven audio technique known to enhance creativity and cognitive abilities. This particular frequency has been demonstrated to stimulate specific areas of the brain, promoting fluent thinking, enhancing problem-solving skills, and fostering a more creative mindset during work and study.

N1 Sleep

A study published in the journal Science Advances has extensively investigated the influence of the N1 stage of sleep on creativity and problem-solving abilities. N1 stage often marked by a moment of relaxation, such as the relaxing of finger muscles. Our app is designed to assist users in waking up during the N1 sleep stage to maximize these benefits.
